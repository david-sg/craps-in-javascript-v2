How to Play

'Come Out Roll' (Initial Roll)

  • Roll a 7 or 11 you win! ($10 bet pays $10)
  • Roll a 2, 3 or 12 you lose (craps)
  • Roll any other number that becomes 'The Point'
  • Once a bet is placed, and a roll made, it can not be changed

'Point Roll' (Subsequent Rolls)

  • Roll 'The Point', you win! ($10 bet pays $10)
  • Roll a 7 and you lose (seven out)
  • Roll any other number, roll again till you hit the point, or you seven out
